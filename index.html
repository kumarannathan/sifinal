My Favorite Movies & Shows

Hi, I'm Kumaran! This is my space dedicated to some of my favorite movies and TV shows. Along with that, you'll find my "Greatest of all time" collectin (GOATs), where you can see my top ten movies and tv shows of all time!

Movies

Fallen Angels

"Fallen Angels" mesmerizes with its stunning visuals and a haunting portrayal of isolation, weaving a mesmerizing tale of lost souls seeking connection amidst the bustling chaos of Hong Kong's neon-lit streets.

Neo-Noir, Romance, Drama

Inception

"Inception" is a mind-bending thrill ride that explores the depths of dreams, seamlessly blending gripping action with a thought-provoking narrative about the power of the subconscious mind.

Thriller, Sci-Fi, Mind-Bending

Fight Club

The unnamed Narrator, an insomniac but otherwise healthy man dissatisfied with his job and lifestyle, attends support groups for problems such as alcoholism and cancer as a substitute for therapy. His efforts are disrupted when another impostor, Marla Singer, joins the same groups.

Drama, Psychological, Thriller

Akira

"Akira" is a masterpiece that blends stunning animation with a dystopian narrative, exploring power, friendship, and the consequences of unchecked ambition in a cyberpunk world.

Cyberpunk, Dystopian, Sci-Fi

Succession

"Succession" hooked me with its intense portrayal of family power struggles and the intricate web of manipulation and ambition. Its characters' complexities and the constant power plays keep me glued to the screen, making it an addictive and thought-provoking watch.

Drama, Satire, Political Intrigue, Family Dynamics

Breaking Bad

"Breaking Bad" enthralls me with its gripping descent into moral ambiguity, following a high school chemistry teacher turned methamphetamine manufacturer. The show's intense character development and unrelenting tension make it an unforgettable exploration of crime and consequence.

Crime, Drama, Thriller, Moral Ambiguity

See My Ranking

Silicon Valley

"Silicon Valley" resonates with me for its hilarious yet insightful take on the tech industry's quirks and challenges. The show's blend of witty humor and clever satire, exploring the highs and lows of startup culture, never fails to entertain and educate.

Comedy, Satire, Tech Industry, Startups
